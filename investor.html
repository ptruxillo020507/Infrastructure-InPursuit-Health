📋 DocSend Setup Guide (10 Minutes)

Follow these steps to connect your documents and start tracking investor engagement:

  • Step 1: Go to docsend.com → Create account → Choose Personal or Standard plan ($10/mo gets you analytics)
  • Step 2: Create a Space called "InPursuit Investor Data Room"
  • Step 3: Upload all 10 documents listed in the sidebar (PDFs, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX — DocSend handles them all)
  • Step 4: For each document, click Share → copy the link (looks like https://docsend.com/view/abc123)
  • Step 5: Open this HTML file in any text editor → Find the DOCSEND_LINKS section near the bottom → Paste each link next to its filename
  • Step 6: Save the file and upload to your website. Done.

Recommended DocSend Settings:

  • "Require email to view" = ON — Investors enter their email before viewing. You see exactly who opened what.
  • "Allow downloading" = OFF for most docs (better tracking) — ON only for the SAFE agreement they need to sign
  • "Passcode protect" = Optional — adds another layer of security for highly sensitive docs
  • "Require NDA" = ON if you have an NDA template — DocSend can require a click-through NDA before viewing

What DocSend Gives You: Real-time alerts when investors open documents. Page-by-page heatmaps showing what they read closely. Time spent per page. Whether they forwarded the link. Download attempts. This intel tells you who's seriously interested and exactly when to follow up.
