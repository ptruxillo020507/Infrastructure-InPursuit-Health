Under the Hood

Meet TETRA Sentinel.

The supervisory AI agent living inside TETRA Aegis. It evaluates and mediates your communications to any remote AI model — whether that's ChatGPT, a clinical AI agent, or any future model. Your AI's AI.

01

Pre-Submission Interception

Every prompt is captured at the client layer before it reaches any external model. No data leaves without passing through TETRA Aegis first — this is an architectural constraint, not a policy.

02

Adaptive Sensitivity Classification

TETRA Sentinel classifies content across multiple risk dimensions — medical, financial, legal, identity, and personal — using fine-tuned models optimized for speed and accuracy on-device.

03

Risk-Scored Decision Engine

Each interaction receives a quantitative risk score. Low-risk prompts pass instantly. Medium-risk prompts are automatically sanitized. High-risk prompts trigger alerts or blocks — all in under 200ms.

04

Transparent Audit & Control

Every decision is logged locally with full provenance. You see exactly what was modified and why. Enterprise deployments get centralized dashboards; consumers get a clean, simple privacy report.
